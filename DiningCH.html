    Lantern Restaurant

    Nov 1
    Modified by Leo Young, a transfer Junior who enjoyes foods

We started with the salt and pepper shrimp which are lightly fried in the shell like soft shell crab. Generous portion. I followed with the smoked chicken which was out of this world.     Jujube

    Oct 28
    Modified by Kaven Brown, a amateur tennis player
    Jujube deserves the 5 star ratings it receives. Despite being in a strip mall, it has a very cozy, well-appointed feel inside. It's a relatively short menu, which I like, but we still had trouble deciding what we wanted because everything sounded so good. We ended up sharing short rib dumplings to start, and shared bulgogi and Kung Pau. Everything was very fresh tasting, with great flavors, and light on the palate. The service was great too, and masks, and covid protocols were well followed.
